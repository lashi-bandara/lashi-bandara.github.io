I am currently a Senior Lecturer in Mathematics at the Deakin Mathematics Research and Teaching Lab at Deakin University (Burwood campus).

Priot to that, I was a Lecturer in Mathematics at the Department of Mathematics at Brunel University London.

Before that, I was a postdoc in the Geometry group at the University of Potsdam lead by Christian Bär, funded via the SPP2026 - Geometry at Infinity which is a Special Priority Program of the DFG (Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft/German Reserach Foundation).

Previously, I did a postdoc with Andreas Rosén at Mathematical Sciences at University of Gothenburg in Sweden funded via a Knut and Alice Wallenberg Fellowship.

My PhD supervisor was the late Alan McIntosh. A link to my thesis is here.
