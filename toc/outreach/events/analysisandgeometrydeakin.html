What, Why, Who...

What?

This is a one day conference centered on analysis, geometry and their intersection.

Why?

Global analysis is the marriage of analysis, geometry and operator theory. Differential operators encode geometric and topological problems and the analysis of these operators yeild powerful geometric results.

Who?

The aim of this meeting is to bring together participants with an interest in analysis and geometry from within Australia.

This event is open to everyone and in particular,Honours students, PhD students and Postdocs are welcome and invited to attend the event.
